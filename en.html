About Me

Xiaojiang Peng is an associate professor at the College of Big Data and Internet, Shenzhen Technology University. He received his Ph.D. degrees from Southwest Jiaotong University. His research interests include computer vision and deep learning. He has published more than 50 top journal/conference papers (e.g., TIP, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, IJCAI, AAAI). He was an associate professor at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology Chinese Academy of Sciences from 9/10/2017 to 31/10/2020. HHe was a postdoctoral researcher at Idiap, Switzerland from 1/7/2016 to 30/9/2017 where he worked with Prof. Francois Fleuret. e was a postdoctoral researcher at Inria LEAR/THOTH from 1/3/2015 to 30/6/2016 where he worked with Cordelia Schmid. He work mainly focus on affective computing, face recognition, unsupervised/weakly-supervised deep learning, and AI edge computing.
